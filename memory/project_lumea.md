Lumea is an LED light therapy mask brand (G11 model, £249) targeting the UK luxury skincare market.

## Brand Identity (from moodboard PDF)
**Logo:** "Lumea" in custom italic serif + "LIGHT THERAPY" in spaced Montserrat caps below.

**Color Palette (exact hex from moodboard):**
- `#CB9C2C` — primary gold (--gold)
- `#CDA032` — secondary gold (--gold-l)
- `#25211E` — warm dark brown/black (--black, --dark, --text)
- `#FCEA66` — bright accent yellow-gold (--gold-bright)
- `#F8EF7A` — lighter yellow (secondary accent)
- `#F1F2F2` — near-white background (--cream)

**Typography:**
- Primary serif: Cormorant Garamond (Google Fonts — closest free match to "Gangjiem Regular" specified in moodboard)
- Secondary sans: Montserrat (confirmed in moodboard)
- Weights: serif 300–400 italic/roman; sans 300–700

**Mood Statement:** "LUMEA embodies the art of radiant transformation—where advanced light therapy meets timeless luxury, creating an elevated wellness experience defined by elegance, confidence, and glow."

**Visual Direction:** Warm beige/sand tones, silk/satin textures, amber-gold product photography.

## Product: Lumea G11
- 147 LED chips (90 face + 57 neck)
- 4 wavelengths: 415nm (acne), 590nm (glow/redness), 633nm (collagen), 850/1072nm (deep repair)
- 6 modes: Repairing, Rejuvenation, Anti-Aging, Morning Skincare, Anti-Acne, Bedtime
- 5000mAh battery, Type-C USB
- Medical-grade silicone, 4D curved
- 10Hz pulse, 3 brightness levels
- Certifications: CE, UKCA, RoHS, FCC, IEC 60601, ISO 13485, SGS
- Price: £249, free UK delivery, 30-day guarantee, 1-year warranty

- `index.html` — main homepage
